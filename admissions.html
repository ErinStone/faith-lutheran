From The Principal

Thank you for taking the time to visit Faith Lutheran School. I am so pleased to be part of such an amazing community of people who who are committed to our students and their families. It is a day and age where the choice you make for your child's education can play a huge role in the character they display now as children and later as adults. We strive for excellence in education and evaluate our practices, curriculum, and approaches often. However, one thing has remained the same at Faith for the last 62 years - we teach children about Jesus and the great love he has for our students. He laid down his life for the sake of redeeming us and therefore we can have a personal relationship with Him.

At Faith, all of our teachers are dedicated to ministering to children daily. Regardless of background, ability, behavior, ethnicity, or any other factor, we love the children in our school and pray that God work through us to teach and lead them.

I invite you to come and visit us to experience our community for yourself. With support from Lutheran Churches around town we are making a difference in our community. Thank you for considering Faith for your child.

God's blessings on you and your family,
Esther Nicholas, Principal
