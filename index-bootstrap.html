Welcome

Welcome to Faith Lutheran School. A member of the Tucson community since 1952, Faith strives to develop well-rounded, caring community leaders with academic integrity in a nurturing environment. Over the years the school has earned praise for its continuous improvement in student test scores and consistently high levels of parent satisfaction. In 2014 Faith Lutheran School earned the National School Choice Leadership Award in recognition of our tireless and continuous commitment of excellence in education for our children.

We invite you to review the department pages as well as our Mission Statement and School History. These will provide you insights into both our programs and our school values. Feel free to contact us through the website or by telephone if you have any questions about our programs or would like to take a tour of our campus.
